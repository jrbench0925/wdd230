Site Name - Blackfoot Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Purpose

Provides information about businesses, events, and culture in the Greater Blackfoot area.

Established in 1952, the Greater Blackfoot Area Chamber of Commerce is a non-profit organization committed to enhancing the Blackfoot community's quality of life by promoting business prosperity. The Chamber fosters community relationships Through a variety of community events.

Scenarios

  • Provides business related resources.
  • Provides a central location to see community events.
  • Provides some basic history of the area.
  • Lists contact information and Chamber board members.
